## Costs of Transportations

Rodrigue and Comtois summarized the factors that jointly decide the costs and rates for all transportations.

1. Geography
2. Type of product
3. Economies of scale
4. Energy
5. Trade imbalances
6. Infrastrctures
7. Mode
8. Competition and regulation
